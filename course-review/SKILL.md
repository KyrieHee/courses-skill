# Course Review

## Workflow

1. Confirm what has been learned and what evidence exists: notes, exercises, quiz results, teacher comments.
2. Build a compact knowledge map.
3. Convert notes into active recall prompts.
4. Add mixed practice and spaced repetition.
5. Identify remaining gaps and route to tutoring, practice, exam prep, or mistake repair.

## Review Rules

- Avoid passive rereading as the main method.
- For facts and vocabulary, use spaced repetition and retrieval.
- For methods, review by problem type and decision cues.
- For reading/writing, review through text evidence, paragraph structure, and revision.
- For university courses, include synthesis questions and cumulative problem selection.

## Output

Return:

- Knowledge map.
- Recall question set.
- 30/60/90-minute review plan.
- Spaced review schedule.
- Gap list and next stage.
